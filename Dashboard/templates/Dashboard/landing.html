About CoG

We are a simplistic pooling of many information sources with crowdsourced expandability.

Donut UI

With Cog, you can visualize your schedule on a 12-hour time wheel. The dynamic clock will slowly eat away at your time, maximizing productivity and efficiency, while minimizing procrastination.

Smart Manager

Cog automatically estimates the lengths of your tasks and arranges them for you. With our unique Donut UI, you can see how long your tasks will take and make maximum use of your time!

Easy Scheduling

Scheduling itself is a stressful process. Let Cog take care of it for you. You can turn your long to-do lists into organized schedules, and also import from Google Calendars and other sources.
